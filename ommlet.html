BREAKFAST

OMELETTE

    Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • chopped tomatoes
  • diced onion
  • 1/4 tablespoon salt
  • 1/4 tablespoon blackpaper
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil

    preparations

  1. Break the eggs into a bowl
  2. Heat the oil in a pan in low heat
  3. Add salt and blackpaper to the eggs and whisk them together.
  4. Add the chopped tomatoes and diced onions to the egg mixture and whisk them together
  5. pour the egg mixture to the pan and cook each side for four minutes
  6. serve while still hot

TOASTED BREAD AND EGGS

    Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp cooking oil
  • 5 slices of bread (brown)

    preparations

  1. Break the eggs into a bowl
  2. heat the pan for one minute in low heat
  3. add salt to the eggs and whisk them together
  4. add cooking oil to the pan then heat for one minute
  5. coat the slices of bread with the egg mixture
  6. then place the egg-soaked slices gently onto the pan.
  7. cook each of the sides for one minute.
  8. serve while hot with prefered beverage
